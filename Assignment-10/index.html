1950's Design Trends



Atomic design was popular in 1950's design that was inspired by space, atomic science, and human achievement. Designs at the time included things like stars, stripes, checks and polka dots.



Bold designs like stars, stripes, checks and polka dots became popular. With atomic design becoming popular, the infamous "Boomerang" pattern took off.




These popular atomic design patterns made their way into fabric as well. Neutral solids, floral prints, and abstract designs were popular for fabrics in the 1950s.
